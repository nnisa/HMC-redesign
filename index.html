HEALTH PRECAUTIONS DURING DUST STORMS

An expert on respiratory care from Hamad Medical Corporation (HMC) advises to take basic health and safety precautions during the dusty season in Qatar. Dust storms, which come with the onset of spring in Doha and decrease visibility for motorists, also pose health hazards such as sinus allergies and respiratory infections.
